About IASC

Instrumentation Automation Surveillance & Communication Sector Skill Council (IASC SSC) is an Autonomous Industry lead Not for Profit Organization under the aegis of Ministry of Skill Development & Entrepreneurship (MSDE), Government of India. It is promoted by the Industry with financial support from National Skill Development Corporation. The IASC SSC collaborates with multiple Ministries, Skilling Organizations, and Technical Institutes to foster skill development among India‚Äôs youth and address the gap between manpower demand and supply.



Our Mission


At IASC (Instrumentation Automation Surveillance & Communication), our mission is to empower individuals and organizations by providing cutting-edge skills development programs tailored to meet the evolving demands of the global workforce. We are dedicated to fostering a culture of continuous learning and growth, ensuring that our clients stay ahead in their respective fields. We strive to: - Deliver high-quality, innovative training solutions that are accessible and adaptable to diverse learning needs. - Bridge the skills gap by offering comprehensive programs that combine theoretical knowledge with practical application. - Cultivate a collaborative learning environment that encourages creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving. - Support career advancement and professional growth through personalized learning pathways and expert guidance. - Contribute to the development of a skilled and competitive workforce that drives economic growth and social progress. At IASC, we believe that investing in skills development is the key to unlocking potential and achieving success in an ever-changing world.
